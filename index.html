About Me

Hey there! My name is Krupali Patel.

I am currently a second-year student at York University studying computer science (BSc Honors). Over the past year, I have gained experience working with JavaScript, CSS, HTML, Java, and Andriod App development through assignments and various personal projects. Also, I enjoy working with many tools and platforms for coding, with some of them being Eclipse, IntelliJ, Android Studio Code, and Visual Studio Code.

These art projects help bring out my creative side! I also love spending time with my family and watching movies (I just started watching all the Marvel movies)!

In the future, I hope to gain more experience in my field through working on many more projects and cooperating with others who share similar interests as me.
